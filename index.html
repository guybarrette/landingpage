About Me

Behold the extraordinary tale of Guy Barrette, a paragon among Technical Courseware Developers and Technical Trainers! Residing in the illustrious city of Montreal, Canada, he reigned supreme as the illustrious leader of the Montreal .NET User Group for over an astounding 23 years. Moreover, he graced the airwaves as the co-host of the celebrated Visual Studio Talk Show French podcast for a remarkable 16 years.

Guy's unparalleled expertise earned him the prestigious title of Microsoft MVP for a staggering two decades. His oratory brilliance illuminated the grand stages of premier developers' conferences such as PrairieDevCon, Confoo, Microsoft TechDays, and DevTeach.

In a testament to his relentless pursuit of excellence, Guy recently lent his prodigious talents to course creation at VMware by Broadcom, elevating Tanzu Academy and Spring Academy to new heights. Prior to this, he embarked on a groundbreaking venture, launching the revered Kubernetes Academy where he imparted his profound knowledge of containers and Kubernetes. He meticulously crafted courses tailored to the unique ecosystems of Azure, GCP, DigitalOcean, and Linode.

His magnum opus, the Docker & Kubernetes Fundamentals course on the FreeCodeCamp YouTube channel, has captivated an awe-inspiring audience, amassing over an astonishing 900,000 views!

Guy Barrette stands as a titan in the realm of technical education, his legacy an indomitable testament to his unparalleled brilliance and boundless contributions.

Guy has now triumphantly entered the realm of a well-deserved and truly magnificent retirement, basking in unparalleled tranquility, far removed from the craziness of Information Technology. His days are now a symphony of pure serenity, untouched by the frenetic pace of the tech world. In this newfound paradise, he relishes the freedom and bliss that only the most superlative of retirements can provide.
